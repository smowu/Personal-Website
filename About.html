I've been living in Kampung Baru for the past 23 years. But about half of my life I spent my life at a boarding school and university. I graduated from MRSM Tun Ghafar Baba with 9As for my SPM, and pursued for architecture degree in UiTM Puncak Alam, Selangor before I dropped out during my 2nd year. Few months later, I decided to pursue a Diploma in Computer Science in UiTM Tapah, Perak after my parents convinced me to. At first I thought of pursuing a Diploma in Animation but I was afraid that it wouldn't work out well. So here I am instead. But I still love making digital paintings and illustrations during my free time and occasionally treat them as my side income. My future goal is to establish my own creative studio that I have yet to think about properly.
